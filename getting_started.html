Getting started

Here is what should be done so that you can get started prescribing medications.

  1. New prescribers - Add all the people that will be able to prescribe medications.
  2. New pharmacists - Add all the people that will be permitted to fill prescriptions. Note that nurses and techs (if legally permitted) should be entered here if they will be filling prescriptions. Additionally, any prescribers should have an entry here if they intend to fill prescriptions as well.
  3. Build a formulary - Enter all the medicines that will be in your formulary. Note that entering actual inventory details (expiration, lot number, etc.) will come in the next step.
  4. Enter inventory - The final step before you will be ready for prescribing.
